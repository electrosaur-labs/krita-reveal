Controls
Colors 6
Target number of ink colors — the engine may produce a few more or fewer depending on image content and ghost removal.
Ghost Removal 0.5%
Eliminate ghost screens — colors covering less than this % of the image get merged into their nearest neighbor.
Dust Removal 0 px
Remove isolated pixel clusters smaller than this radius — cleans up stray dots that would print as visible defects.
Shadow Floor 0%
Minimum ink density per screen — faint mask areas below this threshold get boosted to a printable level. 0% = no clamping.
